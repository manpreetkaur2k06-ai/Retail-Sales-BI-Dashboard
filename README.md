# Retail Sales & Business Intelligence Dashboard

## Project Overview
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze retail sales performance, product categories, outlet performance, and customer trends. The project enables data-driven business decisions through dynamic visualizations and KPI tracking.

## Business Objective
- Monitor key sales KPIs
- Identify top-performing products
- Evaluate outlet performance
- Analyze customer preferences
- Support strategic business decisions

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- DAX

## KPIs
- Total Sales
- Average Sales
- Total Items
- Average Rating

## Dashboard Features
- Sales by Product Category
- Sales by Outlet Type
- Sales by Outlet Location
- Sales Trend Analysis
- Outlet Performance Summary
- Interactive Filters

## Business Insights

1. Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods emerged as the highest-selling product categories, indicating strong and consistent customer demand for essential grocery items.

2. Supermarket Type 1 generated the highest overall sales, making it the most significant contributor to Blinkit's revenue among all outlet formats.

3. Tier 3 locations outperformed Tier 1 and Tier 2 in total sales, suggesting higher customer demand and revenue potential in smaller cities.

4. Medium-sized outlets contributed the largest share of revenue, demonstrating an optimal balance between operational capacity and customer reach.

5. Low Fat products accounted for a larger proportion of sales compared to Regular products, indicating a growing consumer preference for healthier food options.

6. Older outlets consistently generated higher sales than newly established outlets, reflecting stronger customer loyalty and operational maturity.

7. The sales distribution across outlet types highlighted significant performance differences, suggesting that outlet format has a direct impact on revenue generation.

8. The dashboard's interactive filters revealed varying sales patterns across product categories, outlet sizes, and locations, enabling targeted business analysis and decision-making.

9. The average customer rating remained consistently positive across outlets, indicating satisfactory customer experience and service quality.

10. Interactive KPI monitoring enabled quick evaluation of revenue, product performance, outlet efficiency, and customer satisfaction from a single executive dashboard.

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase inventory and promotional focus for high-performing product categories such as Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods.

2. Prioritize expansion in Tier 3 cities, where outlets demonstrate stronger revenue performance and customer demand.

3. Replicate the operational strategies of Supermarket Type 1 across other outlet formats to improve overall sales performance.

4. Increase the availability and marketing of Low Fat products to capitalize on changing consumer preferences.

5. Analyze the practices of older, high-performing outlets and implement similar strategies in newly established stores to accelerate their growth.

6. Use dashboard insights regularly to optimize inventory allocation, promotional campaigns, and outlet-level decision-making.
